Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and services.

How does the hourly billing work?
Hourly billing charges are calculated based on the actual time your vehicle is parked. You're billed in 15-minute increments after the first hour. The system automatically tracks your entry and exit times for accurate billing.
Can I change my plan anytime?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Daily and monthly plans offer the best flexibility for regular users.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments, net banking, and popular digital wallets like Paytm, PhonePe, and Google Pay. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
Is there a refund policy?
Yes, we offer refunds for unused time. If you leave early, you'll be charged only for the actual time used. Monthly plans have a 7-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied.
Do you offer corporate plans?
Absolutely! We provide customized corporate parking solutions for businesses. Contact our sales team for special pricing and features tailored to your organization's needs.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your subscription anytime through your dashboard or by contacting our support team. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period with no additional charges.
