About ParkTech

We're on a mission to make parking the least stressful part of your day.

Our Mission

Smarter Cities Start with Smarter Parking

We are a next-generation technology company focused on building smart parking solutions for modern cities. Our mission is to simplify urban parking using innovative digital systems that save time, reduce traffic congestion, and improve everyday mobility.

Founded with a vision to bring innovation into traditional parking systems, we use advanced technologies like IoT, Artificial Intelligence, and cloud computing to create intelligent and scalable solutions. Our platform is designed to provide real-time insights, seamless booking, and efficient parking management for users and businesses.

We are continuously evolving with the latest trends in smart city development, ensuring that our solutions are not only efficient but also future-ready. Our system is built to adapt, grow, and support the increasing demands of urban infrastructure.

Our Journey

My Journey

1

🌆 November 2025 — Problem Discovery

During city analysis in Pune, we observed major parking issues in high-traffic areas like Pune Railway Station and Shreemant Dagdusheth Halwai Ganpati Temple. This inspired us to find a smart solution for managing parking efficiently.

2

🔍 December 2025 — Research & Market Study

We explored existing solutions through online platforms and found that smart parking technologies were limited and not widely implemented, especially in local areas.

3

🏢 January 2026 — Industry Exposure

Joined a business-oriented company where we were trained to build real-time projects after completing each programming language module, giving us practical development experience.

4

📚 February 2026 — Learning & Data Collection

Alongside training in APIs and frontend development, we collected research data and explored technical approaches to build a smart parking system.

5

💡 March 2026 — Idea Implementation

As part of a frontend task, we presented this Smart Parking System idea and started implementing it as a real-time project using modern web technologies.

6

🚀 2026 — Solution for Smart Cities

This project is developed to solve parking problems across cities by providing smart, scalable, and user-friendly parking solutions for the future.
